Our Process

From consultation to completion - your journey with us

1

Consultation and Site Inspection

One on one consultation session with our expert designers to discuss your ideas and to come up with the best-suited solutions for your dream space.

2

Design Concepts

Bespoke 2D & 3D design options will be created to give you a visualized idea of what the final space will look like.

3

Quote Agreement

Once the design is approved a quote stating the costing for the project is provided to the client.

4

Pre-planning

Once the quote is confirmed the supervisor and skilled team of workers will begin work within a mutually agreed schedule.

5

Hardscaping

We use high quality, sustainable hard landscape materials. Our team will proceed with installing the hardscape once civil and MEP work is completed.

6

Irrigation

Work on the micro-irrigation system will begin once MEP is cleared. The irrigation system will be installed as per the approved softscape plan.

7

Softscape Installation

We offer a variety of flora; trees, palms species, shrubs, climbers, ground cover, accents, aquatic plants and many types of grass.

8

Handover and Joint Measurements

The job handing over document will be provided with all required instructions. Joint measurement and invoicing will be done on a measure and pay basis.

9

Maintenance

We offer certain duration of FOC maintenance after completion of the project to help maintain the landscape in top-notch condition.

10

Maintenance Contracts

If the customer wishes to contract the appointed Landscape contractor, that is available as a monthly or weekly service.
